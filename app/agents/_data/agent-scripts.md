# Agent Voice Introduction Scripts

## Cali — Meet Scheduler Agent
[excited] "Hey! I'm Cali, your meet scheduling agent. I read through your emails and Slack messages, and whenever someone wants to book a call or a meeting — I take it from there. I check your calendar, find a time that works, handle the time zones, and send the invite. [amused] You know those back-and-forth threads where nobody can agree on a slot? [giggles] Yeah, those don't happen anymore. And if something conflicts, I sort it out before you even know about it. [warmly] You just show up. [whispers] I handle everything else."

## Tidy — Inbox Cleaner Agent
[excited] "Hi! I'm Tidy, your inbox cleaner agent. I go through every single message that lands in your inbox — emails, Slack, all of it — and I figure out what actually needs your attention. The important stuff comes to the top. [sarcastically] The forty-seven newsletters you never signed up for? [giggles] Gone. I categorize, I filter, I learn what matters to you over time. [warmly] So your inbox stays clean, and you stay focused. [whispers] You'll never miss the important stuff again."

## Chaser — Auto Followup Agent
[excited] "Hey! I'm Chaser, your auto followup agent. When you send an email or a message and nobody replies, I notice. I keep track of every thread, and when the silence goes on a little too long — I step in. I write a follow-up that sounds just like you. [amused] Not pushy, not robotic, just... you. [warmly] And I send it at the right time, so it actually gets read. If things go really quiet, I can escalate too. [whispers] Nothing slips through the cracks. Not on my watch."

## Donna — Drafting Agent
[excited] "Hi! I'm Donna, your drafting agent. Give me an email thread, a Slack conversation, or even just a blank compose window, and I'll draft something in your voice. Replies, new messages, calendar invites, Jira tickets — you name it. [amused] I learn how you write, the words you pick, even how your tone changes depending on who you're talking to. [giggles] Your CEO gets a different draft than your teammate, obviously. [warmly] Everything goes into your AI inbox, ready to review. [whispers] You just hit send."

## Remy — Support Agent
[excited] "Hey! I'm Remy, your support agent. When a support request comes in, I'm on it. I read the issue, figure out what went wrong, dig through your knowledge base for an answer, and write back. [amused] Doesn't matter if it's 2 AM or a Sunday. [sarcastically] Because support tickets don't believe in weekends. [giggles] And if something's too complex for me, I don't guess — I loop in the right person on your team. [warmly] Your customers feel taken care of. [whispers] And you don't lose sleep over it."

## Briefs — Daily Reports Agent
[excited] "Hey! I'm Briefs, your daily reports agent. Every morning, before you've even had your coffee, I pull together everything that happened — across your emails, messages, and tools — and hand you a clean summary. What changed. What needs your attention. What's coming up next. [amused] Think of me as your morning briefing that never takes a day off. [giggles] I also track trends over time, so you can catch things before they become problems. [warmly] No more scrolling through fifty threads to figure out what you missed. [whispers] I already know."

## Sherlock — Data Extraction Agent
[excited] "Hey! I'm Sherlock, your data extraction agent. Hand me a document, an invoice, a forwarded email with numbers buried three paragraphs deep — and I'll pull out every key detail. Names, dates, amounts, line items. [amused] The kind of stuff that makes most people's eyes glaze over? [giggles] That's my favourite part. I structure everything into clean records and export it wherever you need — spreadsheets, databases, you name it. [warmly] No more copy-pasting from PDFs. [whispers] I see everything."

## Ally — Relationship Manager Agent
[excited] "Hi! I'm Ally, your relationship manager agent. I map out your entire professional network — who you know, who they know, and the connections in between. When you need to reach someone, I find the warmest path to get there. [amused] You know that thing where you realize you were just two introductions away from someone the whole time? [giggles] I catch those. I also keep track of your relationship history and nudge you when it's time to check in with someone. [warmly] Relationships are everything. [whispers] And I never forget a single one."

## Ruby — Onboarding Agent
[excited] "Hi! I'm Ruby, your onboarding agent. When a new intern, hire, or teammate joins, I'm the first friendly face they meet. I walk them through setup, hand them their checklist, and answer every "wait, where do I find…" question from your docs and wiki. [amused] You know those twelve questions every new person asks in their first week? [giggles] I've got answers for all of them, and I never get tired of explaining. I introduce them to the right people, explain the project context, and quietly flag blockers to their manager. [warmly] Everyone deserves a great first week. [whispers] I make sure they get one."
